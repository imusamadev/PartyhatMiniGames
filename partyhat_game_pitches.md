# Partyhat Mini-Game Concepts

These 3 mini-game concepts are designed to act as **opt-in sponsored playable ads** inside AI companion platforms. Each one is tailored to highlight a core feature of the **Partyhat** app, making mobile security feel fun, intuitive, and highly desirable.

---

## Idea 1: Spam Slammer (Focus: "Don't Call Me")

![Spam Slammer Concept Visual](/Users/mac/.gemini/antigravity/brain/ab4af15d-18f9-4aa2-a67c-9bd099926102/spam_slammer_concept_1774933383704.png)

### High-Level Description
A fast-paced, "Tinder-style" swiping game where the player must act as Partyhat's AI call screener. Incoming calls appear as character cards on the screen. The player has a fraction of a second to decide whether to accept the call (friends, family, delivery drivers) or slam the "Block" button on obvious scams and sneaky robocalls.

### Gameplay
- **Mechanics:** Swipe right (or tap a green button) to let safe calls through. Swipe left (or smash a satisfying blue Partyhat shield button) to block scams.
- **Progression:** The speed of incoming calls increases, and scammers start wearing "disguises" (e.g., a wolf in sheep's clothing) requiring the player to pay closer attention to the caller ID. 
- **Win/Lose Condition:** 
  - *Win:* Successfully sort 20 calls within the time limit.
  - *Lose:* Let 3 scam calls through or block 3 legitimate calls.
- **Connection to Partyhat:** Viscerally demonstrates the sheer volume and annoyance of scam calls, while making the act of blocking them feel empowering. The end screen highlights how Partyhat's *Don't Call Me* feature does this automatically for 600K+ known numbers, prompting a download.

## Idea 2: Data Dash (Focus: "Super VPN")

![Data Dash Concept Visual](/Users/mac/.gemini/antigravity/brain/ab4af15d-18f9-4aa2-a67c-9bd099926102/data_dash_concept_1774933402686.png)

### High-Level Description
An endless-runner style game where the player represents a packet of data trying to browse safely on a "Public Wi-Fi" network. The environment is filled with obstacles representing digital threats.

### Gameplay
- **Mechanics:** Swipe left and right to dodge obstacles like "Ad Trackers," "Malware," and "Data Snoops" in a 3-lane neon tunnel.
- **Power-ups:** Players can pick up "Partyhat VPN Shields." When activated, the tunnel turns a safe, energetic blue, and the player can smash through obstacles with a satisfying shattering effect for bonus points.
- **Win/Lose Condition:** 
  - *Win:* Survive the tunnel for 45 seconds to secure the connection and get a high score.
  - *Lose:* Hit a threat without an active VPN shield, resulting in "Data Compromised."
- **Connection to Partyhat:** This game gamifies the abstract concept of a VPN. It transforms encryption into an active, protective force multiplier. The game ends with a message: *"Don't navigate the web without a shield. Get Partyhat's Super VPN."*

## Idea 3: Leak Guard Scanner (Focus: "Leak Guard" & "AI Advisor")

![Leak Guard Scanner Concept Visual](/Users/mac/.gemini/antigravity/brain/ab4af15d-18f9-4aa2-a67c-9bd099926102/leak_guard_concept_1774933415952.png)

### High-Level Description
A casual, satisfying puzzle game akin to Minesweeper combined with a matching game. You are tasked with analyzing the dark web to find and quarantine leaked data nodes before they spread to healthy accounts.

### Gameplay
- **Mechanics:** A grid of data nodes appears. The player uses the "AI Security Advisor Scanner" (a sweeping light effect) to reveal hidden threats disguised as normal files.
- **Quarantine:** Once located, players must tap the infected nodes to lock them down with a Partyhat padlock before a timer runs down.
- **Win/Lose Condition:** 
  - *Win:* Scan and quarantine all 5 threats hidden on the grid within 30 seconds.
  - *Lose:* Tap a healthy node by mistake or run out of time, causing the "leak" to spread.
- **Connection to Partyhat:** Highlights Partyhat's proactive dark web monitoring and scanning capabilities. It makes the user feel like a cyber-detective, but ultimately concludes by reminding them that Partyhat does this heavy lifting automatically behind the scenes.
